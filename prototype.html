Personal Health Records (PHR)

Users can create and manage their comprehensive health profiles, including medical history, prescriptions, and appointments.

User Registration and Activation

Seamless registration and activation process for users, ensuring a smooth onboarding experience and quick access to the SmartHealth Hub platform.

Appointment Scheduling

Users can schedule, modify, and cancel appointments with healthcare providers, streamlining the booking process.

Symptom Checker

An interactive tool that assists users in evaluating their symptoms and provides initial guidance on potential health issues, empowering users to make informed decisions about their healthcare.

Medication Reminders

Automated reminders for users to take their prescribed medications, enhancing medication adherence and overall treatment effectiveness.

E-Prescriptions

Healthcare providers can electronically prescribe medications, reducing errors and improving the efficiency of the prescription process.

Health Forums

Community-driven forums for users to discuss health-related topics, share experiences, and seek advice from peers or healthcare professionals.

Health Analytics Dashboard

Data-driven insights for both users and healthcare providers, aiding in personalized health recommendations and preventive care.

Secure Messaging

Encrypted messaging for communication between users and healthcare providers, ensuring secure and confidential information exchange.

Medical Resource Center

A centralized repository of health-related articles, resources, and tips to educate users on various health topics.

Incident Response Coordination

Coordinated responses to health-related incidents or emergencies, involving collaboration with various roles within the SmartHealth Hub ecosystem to ensure a swift and effective resolution.

Administrative Dashboard

Admin functionalities for overseeing user data, managing healthcare provider profiles, and generating reports on health trends and user engagement.

Medication Dispensation Records

Pharmacists maintain records of medication dispensation, creating a comprehensive history that can be referenced for patient care and compliance monitoring.
